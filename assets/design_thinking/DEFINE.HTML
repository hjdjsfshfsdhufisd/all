DIFINE

In design thinking, "define" is the phase where the team gathered during the empathize phase to articulate the problem clearly. This step involves framing the problem statement in a way that is specific, user-centered, and solvable. The goal is to create a clear definition of the challenges that need to be addressed, which sets the foundation for ideation and subsequent stages of the design process.
